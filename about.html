About Me

Hi! I'm a creative person with technophile leanings. You can usually find me in front of a computer, playing with my kids, or trying to keep my kitten from destroying all my curtains. When I'm not doing one of those things, I enjoy cooking, reading, and falling down the rabbit hole of internet research. There is so much amazing information out there, and I love learning. YouTube and blogs have taught me how to use Photoshop, Illustrator, and a number of other interesting skills.

I recently decided to go back to school to fill any gaps in my knowledge. While I love to create, I also appreciate the less subjective side of coding. My end goal is to work as a web developer, bringing aesthetic beauty and intutitive functionality to clients' websites.
